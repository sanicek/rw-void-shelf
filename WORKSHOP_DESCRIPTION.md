[h1]Void Shelf[/h1]

Imbued with functionality and a little mystery, the Void Shelf lets unwanted clutter fade into oblivion.

[h2]What it does[/h2]

The Void Shelf is a normal storage building. Set its storage filter and priority as you would for any other storage zone or building.

[list]
[*]It starts with an empty storage filter and Low priority.
[*]Only items allowed by its filter will be hauled to it.
[*]On a periodic rare tick, it permanently destroys every item it holds.
[*]There may be a short delay after hauling before the shelf's next rare tick.
[/list]

[h2][b]WARNING: DESTRUCTION IS PERMANENT[/b][/h2]

[b]The shelf discriminates not.[/b] A mistaken filter can destroy valuable items. Once an item is taken by the void, it cannot be recovered.

[h2]Why it exists[/h2]

Void Shelf was created to remove unwanted Combat Extended ammunition clutter. Combat Extended inspired it, but is not required. It can dispose of any items selected through its normal storage settings.

[h2]Compatibility[/h2]

Supports RimWorld 1.5 and 1.6.
